Other Clones

Here are some other places to go if you want to buy or build a TB303 clone.

  • ML-303 a German project, sadly discontinued as of 2004. An inspiration!
  • Acidlab Bassline another German project. Not a 'true clone' but similar sound.
  • Concussor from Analogue Solutions, in the UK. Now available! A 'true clone' with 'DevilFish' mods, but without sequencer.
  • TB3031 from Oakley Sound, the successor to the TB3030, a DIY rack-mount version.
  • TM3030 from Oakely Sound, available 2/2007. Kit, MIDI controlled (no sequencer).
  • Some dude's nifty clone & rack mount version (not for sale)
  • Technology Transplant builds & sells TB303 keyboards, pots, etc. replacement parts.
