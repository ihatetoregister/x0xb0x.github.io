So you want to build a x0xb0x

Congratulations, you are the proud new owner of a box of electronic components. With a bit of effort you can transform these parts into a x0xb0x. By following these instructions, we think you'll be able to get your kit together with a minimum of suffering. Enjoy!

Tech Support?

There is absolutely zero guaranteed tech support with this project. We cant guarantee any response to requests for help. Instead, this manual is intended to be as close to a step-by-step tutorial as possible. Also, it is in your best interest to be cautious and sober when assembling this kit as a mistake can make the synth sound wrong or not work well or perhaps not work at all.

Replacement Parts?

It is highly unlikely that replacement parts will be available in case something goes wrong. However, the BOM (bill of materials) is available, and you can easily order your own replacement parts for common items from any reputable electronics supplier. There is also a list of 'odd transistor' suppliers on the resources page where you may be able to locate replacement parts. Of course, if your kit arrived with parts missing you should contact us immediately!

Corrections & Comments

Corrections and comments on the assembly manual are very much appreciated by us and others. We want to make the manual easy to read and straightforward to follow so that everyone is happy with their kit and none go to waste. If anything in this manual is confusing, overly difficult or just plain wrong, please post a message in the forums
